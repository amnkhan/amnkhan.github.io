What is Silk Of Siam

Silk of Siam is 100% Thai silk, handwoven and hand-dyed. Our scarves, clothing, and silk accessories are of the finest quality, but prices are well below market. Most of our silk is made in a village in northeastern Thailand as part of the OTOP program (where villages are taught a craft in order to be able to earn wages) where fine silk weavers make silk according to traditional patterns for us.

We make silk items for fine fashion houses in Thailand, North America, and Europe, but by bringing them directly to you, we can offer them at far lower prices than otherwise possible.
